About Us

SKYU (pronounced “skew”) is named after SKU, which stands for stock-keeping unit, a term commonly used in the retail industry. Our goal is to revolutionize jobs in the retail industry all whilst helping the brands and retailers that cater to you.

We believe that retail is changing, not dying. We know that our merchandisers can help brands showcase their products effectively and help lay the foundation for the fulfillment of ecommerce in more enhanced retail environments. This in turn can help evolve the overall in-store shopping experience and prepare it for the future to come.

Why brands and retailers partner with us

Drive more sales
We help you create effective and attractive product displays. Why is it important? The better your product or store looks, the more money your customers will spend in-store.
Reduce labor cost
You can optimize your labor costs since there’ll be little need to hire anymore hourly employees. Simply use our network of merchandisers to get your jobs done at a timeframe of your choice.
Get quality results
Completion of every job done by our network of merchandisers will be verified by humans, not computers, ensuring the job was completed to your high standard.
Divide and conquer
Instead of relying on the brands to deliver and display goods to the store, use our platform to reduce costs by dividing tasks and matching them to merchandisers nearby.
Up your digital footprint
Use our platform to meet the growing in-store demand for ecommerce order fulfillment (for retailers with Instacart) or explore the unique ways to promote your store, brand or product on our digital platform.
Unlock new insights
Receive genuine and valuable feedback about your product or store through our network of mystery shoppers.
